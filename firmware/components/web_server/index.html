Calibration

Adjust calibration values. Click "Save Calibration" to apply — the device reboots with the new values.

System Settings

Line frequency and ADC PGA gain (affects current channels only).

Voltage Calibration

Per-phase voltage gain and offset. One set applies to all metering chips.

Current Calibration

Per-channel current gain and offset. Tune gain with a known load; zero offset with no load.

Power Calibration

Per-phase offsets (W / VAR) and power gain.

Phase Calibration

Per-phase angle correction for CT phase shift. Units: degrees.

Energy Offsets

Fixed kWh offset per channel. Use to sync with your utility meter or zero initial energy.

Advanced

Meter constant (combined PL_Constant, sets energy pulse rate), no-load thresholds, and PLL stability gates. Leave at defaults unless you understand each setting.

Firmware Update

Select a firmware binary (.bin) to upload to the device.
